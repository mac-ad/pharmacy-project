Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)!

Some frequently asked questions about our event

  • What is the theme of the 9th APRO GPPed Program?

    The theme of the 9th APRO GPPed Program is "From Classroom to Practice." This theme emphasizes the crucial transition that pharmacy students and professionals undergo from theoretical learning in academic settings to practical application in real-life healthcare scenarios. It underscores the importance of hands-on experience, ethical principles, and practical skills application in shaping competent and ethical pharmacy practitioners who can positively impact patient care outcomes.

  • Who can participate in the GPPed Program?

    The GPPed Program is open to pharmacy students, recent graduates, healthcare professionals, researchers, educators, and anyone interested in advancing their knowledge and skills in good pharmacy practice. Participants from diverse backgrounds and regions are encouraged to join, fostering a rich learning and networking environment during the program.

  • How can I register for the GPPed Program?

    Registration details and procedures for the GPPed Program will be announced soon. Keep an eye on our website for updates regarding registration opening dates, fees, and the registration process. We strive to make the registration process smooth and accessible for all interested participants, ensuring a seamless experience from registration to participation in the program.

  • What are the main highlights of the GPPed Program?

    The main highlights of the GPPed Program include symposiums, workshops, field visits, gala night, international night, and post-event educational tours. These activities are designed to provide participants with a comprehensive learning experience, networking opportunities, cultural exchanges, and insights into current trends and practices in good pharmacy practice across different regions.

  • Is there a registration fee for attending the GPPed Program?

    Yes, there will be a registration fee associated with attending the GPPed Program. The exact fee structure and payment details will be provided in the registration guide once it is released. We aim to offer competitive and reasonable registration fees to ensure accessibility and participation from a diverse range of attendees interested in advancing their pharmacy practice knowledge and skills.
