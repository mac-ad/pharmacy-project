Main Events

Welcome Night

Welcome night will be conducted in the reception hotel with the aim to establish interaction among the guests and the hosts. Different interactive sessions will be conducted with songs and dances as light entertainments.

Symposium Session

Based on the theme “From Classroom to practice”, Proficient speakers will enlighten our participants with their ideas and knowledge regarding the implementation of Good Pharmacy Practice.

Welcome Night

Welcome night will be conducted in the reception hotel with the aim to establish interaction among the guests and the hosts. Different interactive sessions will be conducted with songs and dances as light entertainments.

Workshops

The role of Pharmacists for better implementation of Good Pharmacy Practise, has been described in GPP Guideline published by FiP. The workshops will be focused on one or more missions and roles defined by the guideline to help young pharmacists in understanding and implementing the GPP in practice.

Field Visit

To know more about the scenario of Good pharmacy practice(GPP) in Nepal , participants will be taken on field visits at some hospitals like Dhulikhel hospital,industries and so on. Participants will also be taken to some heritage sites and places that will surely reflect the culture and ethnicity of Nepal.

Gala Night

With the motive of capturing moments, gala night provides participants to dress well. Props and photo booths with themes related to pharmacy and pharmacists will be provided accordingly.

International Night

This event will be conducted in the West Wing Multipurpose Hall of KU.The diversity in the international cultures and traditions will be acknowledged. Cultural dances of different participating countries will be performed. Photo booths are also available.

Godawari Botanical garden

Discover the NBG, once the Royal Botanical Garden, now under Nepal's Department of Plant Resources. Crafted by British architects, it's a hub for plant conservation, research, and recreation. Spread across 82 hectares at the foot of Mt. Phulchowki, with 40 hectares dedicated to themed gardens. Nestled amidst lush evergreen forests, with the enchanting Godawari stream flowing through its heart. Explore this botanical paradise! more

HRDC

In the heart of compassion blooms a sanctuary, a haven where hope intertwines with healing—the Hospital and Rehabilitation Centre for Disabled Children(HRDC). Here, amidst the bustling streets of Kathmandu, lies a beacon of light, founded by the visionary Prof. Ashok K. Banskota and nurtured by the altruistic spirit of the Friends of Disabled. For twenty-six years, this sacred place has stood as a bastion of care, embracing physically disabled children aged sixteen and under, cradling them in warmth and understanding. Click to know more

Pharmaceutical Industry

Nepal's pharmaceutical industry is a whirlwind of innovation and progress! From cities to villages, companies are delivering life-saving medicines with boundless energy. Despite challenges, they're unstoppable, shaping the future of healthcare with quality and affordability. Get ready for a thrilling ride as Nepal's pharmaceutical pioneers lead the way to a healthier tomorrow and join us for an educational visit to one of these dynamic industries and witness the magic firsthand!
